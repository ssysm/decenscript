Welcome to Decenscript.

Decenscript is a decentralized platform for keeping an always trusted transcript for academic institutions using ERC1155 standard (aka. NFT). Each course's grade is minted into an NFT signed with the instructors electronics signature and pack an digital signature in the event of attempted attack.
